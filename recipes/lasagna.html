Lasagna

Lasagna is a dish made of pasta, tomato sauce, cheese, and some other kind of cheese, too.

Lasagna is best cooked in an oven, and served warm. It's very cheesy, so that's nice.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • The other cheese

Steps

  1. Wash pasta sheets in salt water
  2. Boil pasta in a pan
  3. Layer pasta with tomato sauce and the other cheese
  4. Top with the normal cheese
  5. Bake at 500 degrees for 5 minutes
  6. Serve with salad
