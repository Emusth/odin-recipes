Pazooookie

Pazooookie is a dish made of a cookie or brownie base layer with iced-cream on top.

Pazooookie is best cooked in an oven, and served warm with iced-cream on top. It's very sweet, so that's nice.

Ingredients

  • Cookie dough
  • Iced-cream

Steps

  1. Using a flat pan, bake raw cookie/brownie batter in oven
  2. Layer iced-cream on top of hot cookie/brownie
  3. Serve with salad to offset the sugar
